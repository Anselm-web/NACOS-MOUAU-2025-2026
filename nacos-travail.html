About this event

NACOS Travail was a powerful night of worship and intercession organised by the NACOS Director of Socials (Social Oluwa) in collaboration with Umuahia Gospel Hub. The event featured intense worship and prayers for all Nacosite students from 100L to 400L, with guest minister Pst. Manny Ohuche leading the congregation. NACOS President Comr. Ario Benelizer Nwachukwu and DOS Chibuike Isaac Osueke (Oluwa Diamond) anchored the programme at New Colpas Hall on 12th June 2026.
