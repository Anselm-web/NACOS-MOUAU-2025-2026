About this event

The Inauguration Ceremony marked the formal swearing-in of the 2025/2026 NACOS MOUAU executive team, led by Comr. Ario Benelizer Nwachukwu as President-Elect. Held at the Old Colpas Hall on Wednesday 26th November 2025 starting at 12 Noon, the event ushered in a new era of leadership committed to technological excellence, student welfare, and innovation at Michael Okpara University of Agriculture, Umudike.
