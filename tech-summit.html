About this event

TThe NACOS Tech Summit 2026 brought together software engineers, designers, entrepreneurs, and computer science students for a full day of talks, panels, and hands-on workshops. Industry experts from Cowrywise, Google Developer Groups, and Danify International, alongside MOUAU faculty, inspired the next generation of tech professionals. The event, a flagship achievement of the 2025/2026 executive tenure, was graced by the Head of Department, Dr. Samuel Ugboaja (Ph.D), the Matron, Mrs. Prisca Okochi, and the Patron, Dr. Charles Osodeke and some amazing speakers .
