Background

Chukwuma Mac-Davidson is a Computer Science student at Michael Okpara University of Agriculture, Umudike, with a passion for hardware systems and technical infrastructure. He is hands-on, methodical, and dedicated to keeping the department's computing resources in top condition.

As Director of Hardware, his role involves overseeing the maintenance, protection, and operational readiness of all departmental computer hardware, ensuring students always have access to functional technical resources.

Role Description

As the Director of Hardware, I ensure that all departmental computer hardware is properly maintained and operational. My responsibilities include:

Oversee the maintenance and servicing of all departmental hardware.
Coordinate repairs and replacements of faulty equipment.
Maintain an inventory of all hardware assets.
Advise on hardware acquisitions and upgrades.

Key Contributions

Hardware Maintenance Drive

Conducted a full audit and maintenance exercise on departmental computer systems, identifying and resolving faults that had disrupted student access to lab resources.

Equipment Inventory

Created a comprehensive hardware inventory for the department, giving the association and faculty a clear picture of available and needed resources.

Lab Uptime Improvement

Worked with lab technicians to reduce equipment downtime, ensuring students had more reliable access during exam preparation periods.
