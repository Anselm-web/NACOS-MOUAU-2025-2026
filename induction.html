About this event

The 2026 Induction Ceremony officially welcomed a new cohort of computer science students into the NACOS MOUAU family. The event featured four keynote addresses from senior executives, certificate presentations, and a special discussion where Executive leaders shared wisdom with incoming members. A full house of 300+ attendees made it a landmark moment for the association.
